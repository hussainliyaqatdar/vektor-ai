⚠ The Problem

Your data is scattered.
Your decisions are slow.

Modern tech companies generate more data than ever — but getting a single answer still means filing a ticket and waiting days.

Fragmentation
Data lives in a dozen different tools

Product metrics in Mixpanel. Revenue in your CRM. Support trends in Zendesk. Customer feedback somewhere else. Nobody has the full picture without assembling it by hand — if they ever do.

Bottleneck
Every question goes through an analyst

Data requests pile up in Jira. An analyst translates the question, writes SQL, queries the right system, formats the results, and sends back a report — days later, if you're lucky.

Rigidity
Dashboards can't answer new questions

Pre-built dashboards show what someone thought you needed last quarter. The moment business context shifts, you're back to square one. Static charts don't drill down. They don't explain. They just sit there.

Latency
Insights arrive after the window closes

By the time the data story is compiled, reviewed, and presented, the opportunity is gone or the problem has compounded. Teams start making decisions on gut feel because waiting costs more than guessing.

🔒 Security & Compliance

Enterprise-grade security,
built in — not bolted on

Vektor is designed for companies with strict data governance. Your data stays in your infrastructure. We never train on your data.

👤
Role-Based Access Control

Define who can query which sources. Marketing doesn't see revenue data. Engineering doesn't see customer PII. Granular permissions at the team and user level.

🔐
Secure Connectors

All connections are strictly read-only and encrypted in transit. Credentials are stored with enterprise-grade key management. No write access is ever granted.

📝
Full Audit Logs

Every query is logged: who asked, when, which data sources were accessed, and what was returned. Complete traceability for compliance and internal governance.

🏢
On-Premise Deployment

Deploy Vektor entirely within your own infrastructure or private cloud. Your data never leaves your environment. Available on Enterprise plans with VPC support and SSO.
